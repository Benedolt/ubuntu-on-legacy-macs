# Ubuntu 25.10
*updated September 2025*

- ✅ Basically, it just works. 
- ✅ Wayland runs perfectly on this iMac, no notes. AMD and Intel GPUs work.
- ⚙️  *Quality of life*: You might want to `apt install mbpfan` if you replaced the internal HDD with a SSD. Otherwise the HDD-fan will be spinning like crazy, because it's missing a temperature sensor that only Mac HDDs had.
- 🎧 Only sticking point is the sound... Under Ubuntu the sound is a lot "tinnier" than under MacOS, it's really lacking in fullness and base. `Easy Effects` make it a bit better, but it doesn't even get close to MacOS levels.
